Hinoboru Sakaguchi

Here's a time line of Hinoboru Sakaguchi's work:

  • 1984 - The Death trap, first time as game desiner at Square
  • 1987 - Director of Final Fantasy
  • 1988 - Director of Final Fantasy II
  • 1990 - Director of Final Fantasy III
  • 1991 - Director of Final fantasy IV
  • 1994 - Producer and original story of Final Fantasy VI
  • 1995 - Supervisor of Chrono Trigger
  • 1996 - Supervisor of Bahamut Lagoon
  • 1997 - Producer of Final Fantasy VII & Final Fantasy Tactics
  • 1999 - Executive producer of Final Fantasy VIII, Legend of Mana & Parasite Eve 2
  • 2000 - Executive producer of Final Fantasy IX & Hall of Fame Award of the Academy of Interactive Arts & Sciences
  • 2001 - Director of the science-fiction film Final Fantasy: The Spirits Within
  • 2002 - Executive producer of Kingdom Hearts & Final Fantasy XI
  • 2006 - Scenario, lyrics and supervisor of Blue Dragon at Mistwalker
  • 2011 - Director of The Last Story
  • 2014 - Producer of Terra Battle
  • 2015 - Lifetime Achievement Award at the Game Developers Choice Awards
  • 2021 - Producer & scenario of Fantasian

If you are interested, read more about Hinoboru Sakaguchi on his Wikipedia entry.
